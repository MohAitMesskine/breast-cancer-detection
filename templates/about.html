About CancerDetect AI

Advanced deep learning for medical image analysis

Our Technology

This application uses a RegNet (Regional Network) deep learning model, a state-of-the-art convolutional neural network architecture designed for efficient and accurate image classification. The model has been trained on medical imaging data to detect patterns associated with breast cancer.

How It Works

When you upload an image, our AI model analyzes the tissue patterns and cellular structures to classify the image as either benign or malignant. The model provides a confidence score and detailed probability breakdown for transparency.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is important. Images are processed in real-time and are not stored on our servers. All analysis is performed securely, and your medical data remains confidential.

Medical Disclaimer

This tool is designed for educational and research purposes. It should be used as a supplementary tool and NOT as a replacement for professional medical diagnosis. Always consult qualified healthcare professionals for medical decisions.

Model Architecture

Architecture: RegNet-Y 400MF
Task: Binary Classification (Benign vs Malignant)
Input: 224x224 RGB Images
Framework: PyTorch

Usage Guidelines

• Upload clear, high-quality medical images
• Supported formats: PNG, JPEG, JPG
• Maximum file size: 16MB
• For best results, use properly preprocessed medical images
